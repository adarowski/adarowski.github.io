A Little About Me

I’m a husband and daddy.

I’m married to my wonderful wife Erin and have three amazing children who fill my days with joy and panic. We live in Peterborough, New Hampshire with our dog Percy and our cat Hope.

I’m a product designer.

When I’m not being a dad, I design and build user interfaces for Dribbble, a community where designers share what they’re working on. I was previously a part of the excellent product teams at HubSpot, PatientsLikeMe, and Batchbook.

I’m a baseball saberhistorian.

When the kids are asleep and the work is done, I like to write about baseball history from a statistical perspective (while designing and building tools to present my findings). My pet project is The Hall of Stats, an alternate Hall of Fame populated by a mathematical formula.

I recorded a punk rock album.

After taking a decade off from writing and recording music, I released a full-length punk rock album (called “YAY!”) in November 2015.

You can find me on Twitter, Dribbble, GitHub, LinkedIn, Last.fm, Spotify, and BandCamp.
