A Little About Me

I’m a husband and daddy.

I’m married to my wonderful wife Erin and have three amazing children who fill my days with joy and panic. We moved to Peterborough, New Hampshire in the summer of 2014.

I’m a product designer.

When I’m not being a dad, I design and build user interfaces for HubSpot. I work on Social Inbox, our social media monitoring and publishing app.

I’m a baseball saberhistorian.

When the kids are asleep and the work is done, I like to write about baseball history from a statistical perspective (while designing and building tools to present my findings). My pet project is The Hall of Stats, an alternate Hall of Fame populated by a mathematical formula.

You can find me on Twitter, Dribbble, GitHub, LinkedIn, Last.fm, Spotify, and BandCamp.
