A Little About Me

I’m a husband and dad.

I’m married to my wonderful wife Erin and have three amazing children who fill my days with joy and panic. We live in Rehoboth, Massachusetts with three dogs and a cat.

I’m a product designer and developer.

When I’m not being a dad, I’m the Head of User Experience for Sports Reference, LLC. I work on some of my favorite digital products in the world such as Baseball-Reference, FBref, and Stathead. I was previously a part of the excellent product teams at ezCater, Dribbble, HubSpot, PatientsLikeMe, and Batchbook.

I’m a baseball saberhistorian.

When the kids are asleep and the work is done, I like to write about baseball history from a statistical perspective (while designing and building tools to present my findings). My pet project is The Hall of Stats, an alternate Hall of Fame populated by a mathematical formula.

I’m a youth soccer coach.

I began coaching recreational soccer and futsal in 2014 and moved on to travel soccer in 2016. I’m a coach and board member of the Conval Soccer Club. I’m also a Borussia Dortmund fan.

I recorded a punk rock album.

After taking a decade off from writing and recording music, I released a full-length punk rock album (called “YAY!”) in November 2015.

You can find me on Twitter, Dribbble, GitHub, LinkedIn, Last.fm, Spotify, and BandCamp.
